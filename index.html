Mapping America's Agriculture

Where do we raise sheep? How many acres of land are dedicated to vegetables?
Most Americans don't know the answers to these questions, but this map will give you the information you seek!
Select an animal or plant from the dropdown menu, and bubbles representing
the number of animals or acreage of cropland will appear on the map.
Data is from the 2012 Agricultural Census, and provided at the county level. Hold your mouse
over a bubble to find out which county you're looking at and how much is produced there.
